The Masters basketball scene, for men and women, is growing in the UK, Ireland and Europe.

Unfortunately, in this growing phase, not everyone has access to a local team to play for.

The Ronin Masters Basketball Club was formed to give those without a local team or those that might be 9th or 10th on the bench, an opportunity to re-connect with the game of their youth and with others of the same age and ability.

We aim to put together teams in all the age groups, men and women, to compete in all the Masters tournaments in the UK, Ireland and Europe. Currently we're gathering expressions of interest from those who would be like to be involved in any of the age groups/genders at any of the tournaments.
